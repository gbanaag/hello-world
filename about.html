We created this site to help everyone (including ourselves) realize our daily carbon footprint!

Everything contributes to our impact on the planet. One kilo of beef for example, which is around 2.2 pounds, is worth 63 car miles. That plastic cup you just used to drink juice, then threw into one of the recycling bins--was it the green one or the blue one, or was it just the grey trash bin? And, did you wash that cup before tossing it? All of these choices matter.

We hope that raising awareness allows people to make some small lifestyle choices (e.g. eating chicken instead of beef for a day, or choosing to have meatless Mondays)!
